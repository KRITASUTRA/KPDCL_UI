About Us

Bill Sahuliyat – Ab bhariye Bijli ka bill, sirf ek click me!

Bill Sahuliyat is KPDCL’s online bill payment service for consumers. In a world that is increasingly reliant on electricity for its every need, the process of paying your electricity bills should be nothing short of effortless and with "Bill Sahuliyat" simplify the often-complex chore of electricity bill payments, reducing the burden on the consumers.

Bill Sahuliyat helps you to know everything about your electricity account, from knowing previous and current bills, the payments you have made, electricity usage history, connection details and more.

Mission Bill Sahuliyat

The mission is to provide a hassle-free, efficient, and secure way for individuals and businesses to pay their electricity bills. We understand that timely payments are essential to ensuring uninterrupted power supply, and we're dedicated to making this process as easy as possible.

Key Features of Bill Sahuliyat:

Convenience

Going by the name, Bill Sahuliyat is the most convenient and user-friendly way to pay the electricity bill. The interface is designed keeping you in mind ensuring a seamless experience to all, whether you're tech-savvy or new to online bill payments.
The experience is enhanced with the availability of Bill Sahuliyat mobile application. The mobile application We're available 24/7, so you can pay your bills at your convenience, whenever and wherever you need.

Security

Your data and transactions are of utmost importance to us. Bill Sahuliyat employs state-of-the-art security measures to protect your information, ensuring peace of mind with every payment.
Bill Sahuliyat ensures crucial security measures in safeguarding online payment transactions and protecting sensitive financial information.

Bill Payment for Multiple Consumers

With a single login, Bill Sahuliyat gives the power to pay bills for all your near and dears.

We understand that preferences vary. That's why we offer multiple payment options, including credit/debit cards, net banking, UPI, and more, so you can choose what works best for you.
